Alaska Benefits Application PDF

  1. Download the PDF for Alaska's benefit programs.
  2. Fill it out on your computer or phone.
  3. Print it out.
  4. Sign it.
  5. Mail it in to the closest office.
  6. e-mail us with any questions or suggestions.

Code for America has partnered with the Alaska Department of Public Assistance. We've made the PDF to apply for benefits fillable on a computer or phone. The original, un-fillable version is online at on the DHSS webasite.
